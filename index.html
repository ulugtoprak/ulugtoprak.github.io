About Me

Hi, my name is Ulug Toprak, i am 36 and i am a 4th-year student in Institute Of Technology Tallaght Dublin, studying computer science. I have 10 years experience in the hospitality sector and 2 years experience in networking and event management, I also served 15 months in Turkish Armed Forces as a Sgt. Team Captain. After working in the hospitality sector for a considerable number of years I have decided to pursue a career in information technologies and started my 3rd level education in ITT Tallaght. I am thoroughly enjoying the course and looking forward to engaging a career in technology sector which I am fascinated with for a long time. Previously I completed 3.5 years of study towards a degree in agricultural engineering at the University of Ankara in Turkey. My former colleagues and fellow students know me as a very creative person and co-worker who can always come up with solutions to problems needs to be tackled. I do work well on my own and collaborating with others. I live in the tranquil country side of Co.Wicklow with my wife and my 3 kids.
